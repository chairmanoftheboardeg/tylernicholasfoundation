The Dual Mandate

Action for our environment. A voice for our youth.

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation was established on a simple premise: the future of Seychelles depends entirely on the health of our environment and the strength of our youth. One cannot survive without the other.

Operating independently from political boundaries, our mission is to execute grassroots projects that drive tangible change. From coastal preservation initiatives and sustainability tech programs, to direct youth mentorship and community food drives alongside partners like FoodShare Seychelles, we build the frameworks that allow the next generation to thrive.
