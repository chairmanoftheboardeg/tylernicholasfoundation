Who We Are

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation is a dynamic, youth-driven community action group for the Republic of Seychelles. We are a collective of young patriots, advocates, and environmental stewards who believe that true patriotism means rolling up our sleeves to build a better nation.

Driven by a deep love for our islands, we refuse to sit on the sidelines. We serve as the primary voice for the youth, standing alongside government bodies and community leaders to ensure the next generation is always represented.

For the Youth. For the Islands. For Tomorrow.

Message From Our Founder

"I founded the Tyler Nicholas Foundation because I believe in the Republic of Seychelles."

I believe in our people, our environment, our institutions, and most importantly, our future. My love for this nation is not expressed through words alone, but through service. I have always believed that patriotism is more than pride in a flag—it is a commitment to improving the lives of those who call these islands home.

Throughout my journey in youth leadership, advocacy, and public service, I have learned that the future of a nation is not determined solely by economic growth or political decisions. It is shaped by the values we instill in our young people. Education is not only about mathematics, science, or examinations; it is also about integrity, responsibility, compassion, and civic duty. We must raise a generation that not only succeeds, but cares.

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation was created to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity, between challenges and solutions, and between today's youth and tomorrow's leadership. Our mission is simple: to empower young people, strengthen our communities, protect our islands, and cultivate a generation of leaders who will proudly carry Seychelles forward.

I am proud to serve this nation, and I remain unwavering in my belief that together we can build a stronger, more united, and more prosperous Seychelles.

For the Youth. For the Islands. For Tomorrow.

Tyler Nicholas

Founder & Chairperson
