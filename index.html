A Message From The Chairman

"Philanthropy must evolve beyond awareness. It must become infrastructure."

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation was not built to simply talk about the problems facing Seychelles; it was built to aggressively solve them. During my time in public service, I realized that while government policy is essential for the future, our communities and our youth need tangible help today.

This Foundation is a multi-disciplinary action group. We do not restrict ourselves to one single issue because the challenges facing our islands are deeply interconnected. You cannot solve economic stagnation without addressing digital literacy; you cannot protect our coastal environments without empowering the youth who will inherit them.

Tyler Nicholas

Founder & CEO

Our Methodology

Digital-First.
Sweat Equity Driven.

We bypass traditional bureaucracy. By leveraging the corporate infrastructure of The Tyler Nicholas Group, we execute high-impact community projects with minimal financial overhead.

Agile Execution

Operating independently allows us to identify community gaps and deploy resources or digital tools in a matter of weeks, not years.

Corporate Synergy

Backed by our parent company's network, we secure elite work placements and donate professional digital services that standard charities cannot afford.

Radical Accessibility

Every service, website, educational resource, and placement program we engineer is provided 100% free to the recipient.

Scope of Work

A Holistic National Mandate

True progress requires a multi-sector approach. We mobilize our resources across these core pillars to engineer a comprehensive safety net for the Republic.

Career & Opportunity Placement

We actively source and secure customized work attachments, long-term internships, and international holiday opportunities for Seychellois youth by leveraging our corporate network directly on their behalf.

Apply for Placement →

Environmental Stewardship

Executing grassroots coastal protection, organizing massive youth-led mangrove restoration projects, and providing micro-grants for local eco-innovation and sustainability startups.

Digital Literacy & Enterprise

Through "The Artisan Initiative" and our corporate partners, we provide free websites, digital branding, and coding workshops to local vendors and underprivileged youth to transition Seychelles into a creator economy.

Health & Food Security

Maintaining the National Mental Health Directory and partnering closely with FoodShare Seychelles to manage community food logistics for vulnerable demographics.

Cultural Heritage & Inclusion

Running "The Generations Archive" to digitally preserve the folklore of our elderly, while providing strict advocacy and support networks for marginalized groups.
