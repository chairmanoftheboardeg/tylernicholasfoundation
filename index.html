Our Blueprint

Who We Are

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation is a dynamic, youth-driven community action group acting as a vital shadow-infrastructure for the Republic of Seychelles. We are a collective of young patriots, advocates, and environmental stewards who believe that true patriotism means rolling up our sleeves to build a better nation.

Driven by a deep love for our islands, we refuse to sit on the sidelines. We serve as the primary voice for the youth, standing alongside government bodies and community leaders to ensure the next generation is always represented. For the Youth. For the Islands. For Tomorrow.

A Message from the Founder

"I founded this organization because I am a patriot. I love the Republic of Seychelles, and my belief in the future of this country is absolute—our economy, our environment, our politics, and the future generations to come. As a Youth Ambassador, UN Delegate, and advocate, I believe true education reform means understanding that values, integrity, and civic duty are just as critical as learning mathematics. We must raise citizens who care. I love serving this nation, and through this foundation, we will build the leaders of tomorrow."

Tyler Nicholas

Founder & Chairperson
