Initiatives

Action-driven programs for a better Seychelles.

We focus our efforts on the pillars that matter most: empowering our youth, protecting our environment, and feeding our communities.

Youth Leadership & Empowerment

Equipping the next generation of Seychellois leaders. We provide comprehensive mentorship programs, public speaking training, and leadership workshops. Our goal is to give young people the tools, confidence, and platforms they need to actively participate in shaping the future of our islands.

  • 1-on-1 Mentorship
  • Public Speaking & Advocacy Workshops
  • Career Placement Assistance
Join the Initiative

Environmental Stewardship & Sustainability

Protecting the unique biodiversity of Seychelles requires action, not just policy. We mobilize youth-led teams for coastal clean-ups, organize mangrove restorations, and run climate awareness campaigns to ensure our island ecosystems thrive for generations.

  • Youth-Led Coastal Clean-ups
  • Mangrove & Reef Restoration
  • Climate Literacy Campaigns
Join the Initiative

Community Action & Development

We believe in the power of local solutions for local problems. This pillar focuses on mobilizing young people to solve immediate challenges within their districts, fostering a culture of volunteerism, and providing direct support to vulnerable populations.

  • District Volunteer Mobilization
  • Micro-Grants & Support Programs
  • Digital Literacy Training
Join the Initiative

FoodShare Seychelles

As a vital initiative partnered with the Tyler Nicholas Foundation, FoodShare Seychelles tackles food security head-on. We connect essential resources to communities in need across the islands, ensuring efficient distribution and reducing food waste.
