The Manifesto

Built by the youth, for the youth of Seychelles.

Who We Are

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation is a non-profit subsidiary of the Tyler Nicholas Group. We are founded on a simple but powerful premise: true impact doesn't require a massive budget; it requires dedicated people ready to do the work. We are focused entirely on the youth and the environment of Seychelles.

The "Sweat Equity" Model

We don't rely on massive funding to create change. Operating entirely on sweat equity, passion, and manpower, we empower young Seychellois to take immediate, hands-on action in their communities. Your time, energy, and voice are our most valuable resources.
