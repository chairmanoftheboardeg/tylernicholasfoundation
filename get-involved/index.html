Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I have to commit to volunteer?

+

We are highly flexible. You can volunteer for a single weekend event (like a coastal cleanup) or commit to a long-term role (like mentoring for The Artisan Initiative). Let us know your availability during onboarding.

Do I need specific professional qualifications?

+

For technical roles (like The Artisan Initiative), baseline skills are preferred. However, for general community engagement and environmental stewardship, all you need is a willingness to learn and work hard.

How are donations processed?

+

All online donations are capped at 1,500 SCR per transaction for security and compliance. Funds are routed directly into the Foundation's secure banking infrastructure via our payment gateway partner.
